Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 27, 2026

Victor Franchetti Insurance Services respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information submitted through this website, appointment request forms, phone calls, and related communications.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • State of residence
  • Appointment request details
  • Insurance-related information you choose to provide

How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

  • Respond to your request for insurance information
  • Schedule, confirm, or remind you about an appointment
  • Communicate with you by phone, email, or SMS regarding your request
  • Provide information about insurance options that may be available to you
  • Maintain internal records and comply with applicable laws and regulations

SMS/Text Messaging Disclosure

By submitting your information through this website, you consent to receive phone calls and SMS/text messages from Victor Franchetti Insurance Services and its scheduling team regarding your request, appointment scheduling, appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communication.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. Reply STOP to opt out of text messages. Reply HELP for assistance.

We do not use SMS for bulk marketing. Text messaging is used for appointment-related communication and follow-up regarding requests submitted by individuals.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only as needed with licensed insurance professionals, service providers, technology platforms, or carriers when necessary to respond to your request, schedule appointments, provide services, or comply with legal obligations.

Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect personal information. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP. You may also contact us to request updates, corrections, or deletion of your information, subject to legal and business recordkeeping requirements.

Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date.
