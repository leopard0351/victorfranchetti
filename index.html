Why families request a review

Many homeowners want to know what would happen to the mortgage, monthly bills, or family income if something unexpected happened. A short consultation can help clarify available protection options.

Some families also want to understand whether permanent life insurance or indexed universal life insurance may fit longer-term goals. These products are not right for everyone, so the review is designed to explain options, tradeoffs, costs, and eligibility in plain language.

There is no obligation to purchase coverage. Availability and eligibility depend on state, carrier, product, age, health, underwriting, and individual circumstances.

Common questions

Helpful answers before you schedule a consultation.

Is there a cost to request information?

No. Requesting information or scheduling a phone consultation does not require you to purchase insurance.

Why would I receive a text message?

SMS may be used to schedule, confirm, remind, or follow up about an appointment related to your request. You can reply STOP at any time to opt out.

What happens during the consultation?

You can review your goals, ask questions, and learn what insurance options may be available based on your state and circumstances.

What is an indexed universal life insurance review?

An IUL review is a conversation about whether indexed universal life insurance may fit your goals. These policies can include life insurance protection and potential cash value growth, but they are not appropriate for everyone and may involve fees, caps, limits, and underwriting requirements.

Am I guaranteed coverage?

No. Coverage availability, rates, and eligibility may depend on carrier guidelines, state availability, underwriting, age, health, and other factors.
